Publication Ethics

Our Commitment

The Journal of State Management (JSM) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of scholarly publishing, editorial independence, research integrity, and publication ethics.

The Journal follows internationally recognized principles of responsible publishing and expects editors, authors, reviewers, and publishers to uphold the values of honesty, transparency, accountability, fairness, and respect throughout the publication process.

Honesty Transparency Accountability Fairness Respect
Guiding Standards

JSM adheres to internationally accepted standards for editorial decision-making, peer review, and research integrity, including:

COPE Core Practices
Committee on Publication Ethics guidelines for editorial conduct and integrity.
Principles of Transparency and Best Practice in Scholarly Publishing
Jointly developed standards for responsible, transparent scholarly publishing.
International Editorial & Research Integrity Standards
Accepted norms for editorial decision-making, peer review, and research integrity.
Shared Responsibility
All participants in the publication process share responsibility for ensuring that published research is original, accurate, ethical, and trustworthy.
