Corrections

Our Commitment

The Journal is committed to maintaining the accuracy of the scholarly record.

When errors are identified after publication, JSM will publish corrections whenever appropriate.
Corrections May Include
Typographical corrections
Author corrections
Publisher corrections
Corrigenda
Errata
Publication Record

Corrections are linked to the original article and receive their own publication record where appropriate.

Minor typographical errors that do not affect interpretation may not require a formal correction.
