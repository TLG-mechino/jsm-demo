Before You Submit

Authors should ensure that all of the following conditions are met prior to submission:

The manuscript fits the Journal's Aims and Scope.
The manuscript has not been published or submitted elsewhere.
All required submission files are prepared and ready to upload.
Ethical and publication requirements have been satisfied.
The submission checklist has been completed.
