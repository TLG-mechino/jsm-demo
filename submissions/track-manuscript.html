Track Your Manuscript

Overview

Authors may monitor the progress of their manuscript at any time through the online submission system.

Typical Manuscript Statuses
Submitted
Manuscript successfully received by the Editorial Office.
Initial Check
Administrative screening is underway.
With Editor
The manuscript is being evaluated by the Editor-in-Chief or Associate Editor.
Under Review
External peer review is in progress.
Reviews Completed
Reviewer reports have been received and are under editorial evaluation.
Revision Required
Authors are invited to revise the manuscript.
Revised Submission
The revised manuscript has been received and is undergoing further assessment.
Accepted
The manuscript has been formally accepted for publication.
In Production
Copyediting, typesetting, and proof preparation are underway.
Published Online
The article has been published with a DOI.
Author Support

Authors may contact the Editorial Office at any stage of the submission process for assistance regarding:

Submission difficulties Manuscript status Revision procedures Production schedules Publication enquiries
The Editorial Office aims to respond to all enquiries within two business days.
