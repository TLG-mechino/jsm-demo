Abstracting & Indexing

Overview

The Journal of State Management is committed to increasing the international visibility and accessibility of its published research through inclusion in reputable abstracting and indexing services.

Indexing improves the discoverability of articles, enhances citation opportunities, and facilitates scholarly communication across disciplines and regions.
