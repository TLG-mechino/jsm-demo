Editorial Office

Role Overview

The Editorial Office manages the day-to-day operation of the Journal and serves as the primary point of contact for authors, reviewers, editors, and readers.

Working closely with the Editor-in-Chief and Associate Editors, the Editorial Office coordinates manuscript processing, peer review administration, production, publication, and communication with contributors throughout the editorial workflow.

Responsibilities

The Editorial Office is responsible for:

Manuscript Administration
Initial manuscript screening
Author communication
Reviewer invitations
Editorial coordination
Production
Copyediting
Language editing
Typesetting
Proofreading
DOI registration
Online publication
Editorial Support

Website maintenance
Publication scheduling
Indexing support
Metadata management

Research Integrity
Similarity checking
Ethics screening
Publication record maintenance
Correction and retraction management
