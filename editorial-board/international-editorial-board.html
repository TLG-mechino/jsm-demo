International Editorial Board

Role Overview

The International Editorial Board consists of distinguished scholars from universities, research institutes, and public policy organizations around the world. Board members contribute to the Journal by providing strategic academic advice, recommending reviewers, promoting international collaboration, and supporting the Journal's commitment to global scholarly excellence.

The Board reflects JSM's objective of becoming an internationally recognized journal serving both developed and developing countries.
