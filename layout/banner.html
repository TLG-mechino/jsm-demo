The Journal of State Management (ISSN: 2815-6021-E)

The Journal of State Management is a scientific journal published by the Academy of Public Administration and Governance, Vietnam. The journal publishes fully open access scientific articles dealing with the fields of public administration and state management in accordance with the Press Law and Academy regulations, including theoretical and practical issues in state management, administrative reform, public policy, and related areas, information, administrative science and technology, state management.
