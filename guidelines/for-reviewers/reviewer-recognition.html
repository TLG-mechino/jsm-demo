Reviewer Recognition

Our Commitment to Reviewers

The Journal of State Management greatly appreciates the time, expertise, and professional commitment contributed by its reviewers.

Peer review is a voluntary academic service that plays a fundamental role in maintaining the quality and credibility of scholarly communication.

How We Recognize Our Reviewers

JSM acknowledges reviewer contributions through several initiatives:

Annual Reviewer List

Each year, the Journal publishes a list of reviewers who have contributed to the peer review process, subject to their consent.

Certificates of Recognition

Reviewers may request an official certificate acknowledging their reviewing service for the Journal.

Certificates are issued electronically by the Editorial Office.

Outstanding Reviewer Award

Each year, the Editorial Board may recognize reviewers who have demonstrated exceptional performance based on:

Review quality Timeliness Completed reviews Professionalism Constructive feedback

Award recipients are announced on the Journal website.

Editorial Board Opportunities

Outstanding reviewers may be invited to:

Join the Editorial Board.
Serve as Associate Editors.
Participate in Special Issues.
Contribute invited editorials.
Reviewer Benefits

Serving as a reviewer for JSM provides opportunities to:

Contribute to the advancement of public administration scholarship
Enhance academic reputation
Develop editorial expertise
Build international collaborations
Support research quality and integrity
Stay informed about emerging developments in the field
Reviewer Appreciation
Thank You to Our Reviewers

The Editorial Board sincerely thanks all reviewers for their valuable contributions to maintaining the academic quality and international reputation of the Journal of State Management.

Their dedication, professionalism, and scholarly expertise are essential to the success of the Journal and to the advancement of knowledge in public administration, governance, and public policy.
