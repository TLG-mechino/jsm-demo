Article Types

The Journal publishes several categories of scholarly work.

Original Research Articles

Reports of original empirical or theoretical research that contributes substantially to knowledge in public administration, governance, and public policy.

Review Articles

Comprehensive reviews synthesizing current knowledge and identifying future research directions.

Policy Analysis

Evidence-based analyses of contemporary public policy issues, institutional reforms, and governance challenges.

Comparative Studies

Cross-national or cross-regional analyses that generate transferable knowledge for public governance.

Case Studies

Detailed examinations of innovative public sector practices, administrative reforms, or governance experiences.

Invited Editorials

Opinion articles commissioned by the Editor-in-Chief.

Book Reviews

Critical reviews of recently published scholarly books relevant to the Journal's scope.
