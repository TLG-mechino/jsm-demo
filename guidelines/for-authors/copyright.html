Alignment with International Scholarly Publishing Standards

Our Commitment

The Journal of State Management (JSM) has developed its editorial policies, publication procedures, and website infrastructure in accordance with internationally recognized standards for scholarly publishing. The journal is committed to maintaining a transparent, ethical, and high-quality publication environment that supports the dissemination of rigorous research in public administration, governance, public policy, and state management.

Peer Review & Editorial Integrity

JSM operates a formal double-blind peer-review system, supported by clearly published editorial and ethical policies. Comprehensive guidance is provided to authors, reviewers, and editors to ensure consistency, fairness, and academic integrity throughout the publication process. The journal adheres to internationally accepted principles of publication ethics and research integrity, including policies addressing conflicts of interest, plagiarism, corrections, retractions, data transparency, and the responsible use of emerging technologies in scholarly communication.

Discoverability & Metadata

To enhance discoverability and accessibility, JSM assigns Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs) to all published articles and maintains structured article metadata, including titles, abstracts, keywords, author affiliations, and references. English-language metadata is provided for all publications to facilitate international visibility and integration with global scholarly information systems.

Editorial Governance

The journal's editorial governance framework emphasizes transparency, academic independence, and intellectual diversity. Editorial Board members represent a range of institutions, disciplinary backgrounds, and areas of expertise, contributing to the journal's commitment to international scholarly engagement and high editorial standards.

Open Access

JSM also promotes open access to research through a no-fee publication model and the use of internationally recognized licensing arrangements. By maintaining robust editorial processes, ethical safeguards, and international publishing standards, the journal seeks to strengthen its role as a trusted platform for advancing scholarship and evidence-informed governance in both national and international contexts.
