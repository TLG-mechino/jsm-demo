Manuscript Preparation

General Requirement

Manuscripts should be written in clear, concise, and grammatically correct English.

Length

Abstract

The abstract should contain approximately 150–250 words and clearly summarize:

Purpose
Methods
Findings
Originality
Practical implications
Keywords

Provide 4–8 keywords arranged alphabetically.
