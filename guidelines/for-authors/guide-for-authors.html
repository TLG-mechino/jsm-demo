Guide for Authors

General Requirements

Authors should ensure that their manuscript is original, has not been published previously, and is not under consideration elsewhere. Submissions must fall within the scope of the Journal and demonstrate a clear contribution to theory, methodology, policy, or practice.

Before Submitting, Authors Should Confirm That
The manuscript fits the Journal's Aims and Scope
All authors have approved the final version
Ethical requirements have been satisfied
References are complete and accurate
Any conflicts of interest have been disclosed
Funding information has been provided where applicable
The manuscript follows the Journal's formatting requirements
How to Submit
The Journal accepts submissions exclusively through the online manuscript submission system.
