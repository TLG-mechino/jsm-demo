Open Access Policy
Diamond Open Access

The Journal of State Management is a Diamond Open Access journal. All articles are immediately and permanently available online without subscription barriers or access restrictions.

JSM believes that unrestricted access to scholarly knowledge promotes scientific collaboration, evidence-based policymaking, and the global exchange of ideas. Readers may freely read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, and link to the full texts of published articles without prior permission, provided appropriate attribution is given to the original authors.

The Journal does not charge any fees for manuscript submission, peer review, editorial processing, publication, or online access.
