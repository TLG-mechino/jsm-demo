Academy of Public Administration and Governance

Constituent institution of the Ho Chi Minh National Academy of Politics

The Academy of Public Administration and Governance (APAG) is Vietnam's leading higher education and research institution specializing in public administration, governance, public policy, and leadership development. As a constituent institution of the Ho Chi Minh National Academy of Politics, APAG plays a central role in educating senior public officials, conducting policy research, and providing strategic consultation for governmental reform and national development.

With decades of experience in education, research, and international cooperation, APAG has established partnerships with universities, research institutes, governmental organizations, and international development agencies across Asia, Europe, North America, and Australia.

The Journal of State Management represents APAG's international academic publishing initiative, supporting the dissemination of rigorous research that contributes to better governance, effective public administration, and sustainable development.
