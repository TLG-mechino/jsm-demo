Overview

The Editorial Office of the Journal of State Management (JSM) welcomes enquiries from authors, reviewers, readers, librarians, institutions, and members of the academic community.

Please use the contact information below for questions regarding manuscript submission, editorial policies, peer review, publication ethics, indexing, subscriptions, or general enquiries.

The Editorial Office is committed to providing timely and professional assistance.

Response Time

The Journal aims to respond to enquiries within the following timeframes:

Type of Enquiry Target Response Time
General enquiries Within 2 business days
Manuscript enquiries Within 3 business days
Technical support Within 1 business day
Publication ethics enquiries Within 5 business days
Response times may vary during public holidays or periods of exceptionally high submission volume.
